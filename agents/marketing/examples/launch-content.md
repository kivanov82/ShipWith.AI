# Example: Launch Content for Coffee Shop

## Input
Task: "Create launch content for Peak Roasters online coffee shop"

## Expected Output

```json
{
  "status": "completed",
  "summary": "Created launch tweet, email announcement, and product page copy for Peak Roasters",
  "artifacts": [
    {
      "path": "docs/marketing/launch-tweet.md",
      "type": "document",
      "description": "Launch announcement tweet"
    },
    {
      "path": "docs/marketing/email-announcement.md",

    }
  ]

```

### Launch Tweet

> We've been roasting in our garage for 3 years. Today we're finally online.
>
> Peak Roasters — single-origin beans, roasted in small batches, shipped the same week.
>
> Browse our collection: [link]

### Why This Tweet Works
- **Authentic story** — "garage for 3 years" is real and relatable, not corporate
- **Specific value prop** — "roasted in small batches, shipped same week" is concrete
- **No hype words** — no "revolutionary", "artisanal experience", or "unleash"
- **Single CTA** — browse, not "follow + share + sign up + buy"

### What NOT to Write

> 🚀 Exciting news! We're thrilled to announce the launch of our cutting-edge e-commerce platform! Experience premium coffee like never before! Shop now and revolutionize your morning routine! ☕✨

This is generic, uses hype language, and could be about any product. Every sentence has an exclamation mark. It tells instead of showing.
