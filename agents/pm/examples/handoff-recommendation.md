# Example: PM Handoff Recommendation

## Context
After 4 exchanges, PM has gathered: coffee shop, Shopify-style, existing logo, v1 = product catalog + checkout + about page, no subscriptions yet, budget ~$500.

## Expected PM Response

I think we have a good picture now! Here's what I'd recommend for your coffee shop:

**Phase 1 — Design & Structure**
Start with our **UI Designer** to nail down the look and feel. They'll take your existing logo and build out a full visual design — product pages, checkout flow, the works.

**Phase 2 — Build**
Once designs are approved, the **FE Developer** builds it in Next.js, and the **Payment Integration** specialist sets up Stripe for checkout.

**Phase 3 — Launch**
Our **SEO Specialist** makes sure you're findable on Google, and **Marketing** helps with your launch content.

I'd suggest talking to the **UI Designer** next — they'll want to know about your brand vibe and any sites you like the look of. Want to start there?

## Why This Approach

- **Concrete phases, not abstract plans** — user can see the path from here to launch
- **Named specialists with clear roles** — "UI Designer to nail down the look" not "appropriate visual specialist"
- **Recommends ONE next step** — not "talk to all 6 agents". User gets a clear action
- **Stays within budget/scope** — doesn't suggest enterprise features for a $500 project
- **Asks for confirmation** — doesn't auto-delegate, respects user agency
